Sleep Hygiene — Practical Tips

This fixture stresses sentence boundaries, quotes, and small wording shifts. Extraction should include headings and list content.

Top Tips

  • Keep a consistent bedtime and wake time, even on weekends.
  • Limit screens for 60 minutes before sleep.
  • Keep the bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Avoid heavy meals within 2 hours of bedtime.

Note: If you snore loudly or stop breathing during sleep, ask about a sleep evaluation.

A simple routine

Try this: “dim lights → light stretch → warm shower → read a few pages.” Keep it short and repeatable.

FAQ

Q: Is melatonin safe?

A: It may help some people short-term; talk with a clinician if you take other medications.

Q: What if I wake up at night?

A: If you can’t fall back asleep within 20 minutes, get up briefly and do something relaxing.
