Daily Plate Model

  • 1/2 plate: non-starchy vegetables (greens, peppers, broccoli).
  • 1/4 plate: protein (beans, fish, poultry, tofu).
  • 1/4 plate: whole grains or starchy vegetables.

Note: If you have kidney disease, talk to a clinician before increasing protein.

FAQ

Q: Is “low fat” always better?

A: Not necessarily—focus on minimally processed foods and portion size.

Q: Do I need supplements?

A: Many people don’t; ask a clinician if you’re unsure.
