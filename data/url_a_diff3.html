Clinic Visit Checklist — Summer 2026

This content is designed to test multi-paragraph extraction and “small” text differences. Use normalized visible text for comparisons.

Before Your Appointment

  1. Write down your top 3 concerns and when they started.
  2. Bring a list of medications, vitamins, and supplements.
  3. Note any recent tests: imaging, labs, or specialist visits.
  4. Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in paperwork.

Tip: If you use a wearable device, bring recent trends (heart rate, sleep, steps).

During Your Appointment

  • Ask what the diagnosis means in plain language.
  • Confirm next steps and when you should follow up.
  • Discuss side effects for new medications.
  • Request a written plan if available.

After Your Appointment

Review your care plan the same day. If anything is unclear, call the clinic within 48 hours. Keep a folder (paper or digital) for visit summaries and test results.

Contact

Use the patient portal for non-urgent questions. For urgent symptoms, seek immediate care.
